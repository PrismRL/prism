class Scheduler : Object¶

The ‘abstract class’ that Schedulers should inherit from and implement.

__new(self: Scheduler)¶

Constructor for the Scheduler class. Initializes an empty queue and sets the actCount to 0.

add(self: Scheduler, actor: string | Actor)¶

Adds an actor to the scheduler.

Parameters:

actor (string | Actor) – The actor, or special tick, to add.

remove(self: Scheduler, actor: Actor)¶

Removes an actor from the scheduler.

Parameters:

actor (Actor) – The actor to remove.

has(self: Scheduler, actor: Actor) → hasActor: boolean¶

Checks if an actor is in the scheduler.

Parameters:

actor (Actor) – The actor to check.

Returns:

hasActor (boolean) – True if the actor is in the scheduler, false otherwise.

next(self: Scheduler) → next: Actor¶

Returns the next actor to act.

Returns:

next (Actor) – The actor who is next to act.
